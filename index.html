People Analytics Consulting FAQs


What is people analytics?

People analytics uses workforce data to improve decisions about performance, retention, hiring, and employee experience.

How does people analytics help business leaders?

It connects workforce trends to business outcomes, helping leaders reduce risk, improve performance, and make evidence-based decisions.

What makes Velvet Laboratory different?
